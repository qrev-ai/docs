## Why Open Source?

Building in public is one of our primary goal and we truly believe in the power of community to make QRev bigger and better.

Apart from that we want to build Trust; we want to provide users a sense of ownership and 

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Transparency">
    Allows users to inspect the source code, which promotes transparency.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Community Collaboration">
    Collaboration can lead to faster development, bug fixes, and feature enhancements due to the diverse skills and perspectives of contributors.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Cost-Effectiveness">
    This can significantly reduce costs associated with software licensing fees, making it an attractive option for individuals, and businesses.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Avoid Vendor Lock-In">
  Closed source software often ties users to a particular vendor or provider, limiting their options and flexibility
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
